--- layout: answers title: How to change startup disk on Mac OS X? answer:
  1. Turn on, or restart, your Mac. Immediately press and hold the Option key. After a few seconds, the Startup Manager should appear.
  2. To set the default startup volume for your Mac after your computer is running, open System Preferences and choose Startup Disk.
